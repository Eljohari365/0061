Project Overview

A Landmark
Mixed-Use Destination

La Perle Jeddah is a prestigious mixed-use commercial development in the heart of Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. The project was led personally by the Founder and Managing Director of Canadian Builders, who served as Project Manager for the Main Contractor — overseeing the full execution and delivery of this landmark destination.

The development brings together premium restaurants, cafés, high-end office spaces, and a full-footprint basement parking facility — all delivered under a Core & Shell scope within a 20-month construction window and a contract value of SAR 70 Million.
